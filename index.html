Privacy Policy — Chameleon Prop Hunt

Last updated: 2026-06-27

Developer: Jon Mateo

Contact: miappsandgames@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game Chameleon Prop Hunt ("the Game", "we", "us") handles information. Please read it before using the Game.

Summary (Plain Language)

  • The Game does not collect, sell, or share your personal information.
  • All progress (level, XP, coins, statistics, achievements, and settings) is stored only on your device.
  • The Game works fully offline in single-player mode.
  • The optional Online Multiplayer (Beta) uses direct peer-to-peer connections. It shares only the network information necessary to establish the connection and gameplay data during matches. We operate no game servers and maintain no user accounts.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. The Game has no login system, no user accounts, and contains no analytics or advertising SDKs in this version.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The Game stores the following information locally so your progress is saved between sessions:

  • Player level and experience (XP)
  • In-game coins
  • Best score, longest survival, and gameplay statistics
  • Unlocked items and achievements
  • Game settings

This information never leaves your device and is removed if you uninstall the Game or clear its application data.

Online Multiplayer (Optional)

If you choose to use the optional Online Multiplayer (Beta) feature:

  • A direct network connection is established between your device and the host or other connected players.
  • Your in-game position and selected role are transmitted during gameplay so other players can see your character.
  • Your device's IP address is visible to the host, as required for any direct peer-to-peer internet connection.
  • We do not operate multiplayer servers, do not log this information, and do not retain it.

Permissions

The Game requests only the permissions necessary to function, including network access for the optional multiplayer feature. It does not request access to your contacts, location, camera, microphone, photos, or files.

Children's Privacy

The Game does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. No personal information is collected by us.

Third-Party Services

This version of the Game contains no third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking services.

If a future version introduces advertisements, in-app purchases, analytics, or similar services, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those features are released and will identify the relevant third-party providers and their data practices.

Data Security

Because we do not transmit or store personal information, there is no personal data for us to secure on our servers. Game progress stored on your device is protected by your device's normal application sandbox and operating system security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when changes were made. Continued use of the Game after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
